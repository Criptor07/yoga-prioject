Step1.

Warm-up exercises before yoga enhance blood flow into the muscles. It helps muscles to fulfill the need at the physical level before proceeding into the yoga practice.


Step2.

As we begin to move our body parts in a certain direction, the muscular movements direct blood to deliver ample oxygen to the brain. It makes the practitioner more attentive and focused during the Yoga practice.


Step3.

Warm-up yoga stretches raises the blood circulation and makes the muscle contract and relax frequently as long as the practitioner warms up. This makes muscles follow the movements more softly.


Step4.

It is essential to achieve decent flexibility before jumping into the yoga asanas practice. So, warming up yoga helps in allowing some room to extends body movements.
